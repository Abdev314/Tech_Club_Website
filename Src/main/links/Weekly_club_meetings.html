Weekly Club Meetings

Every week, our tech club holds engaging and interactive meetings in a dedicated room where students from various backgrounds come together. These sessions provide a platform to learn, share, and collaborate on technology-related topics such as software development, AI, blockchain, and business-tech. Each meeting is designed to be informative and hands-on, with discussions, project showcases, and networking opportunities that inspire creativity and skill development. With a large number of enthusiastic students attending regularly, our weekly meetings have become a cornerstone for learning and community building in the club.

Join us to experiment, build, and shape the future!
